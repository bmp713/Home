PROFILE

I see software as the most powerful tool we have ever created to communicate ideas. No matter how amazing an idea might be, if you can't present it effectively nobody will care. I believe that the key to innovation is developing a strong artistic intuition by exposing oneself to a wide variety of creative disciplines, both technical and non-technical.

I am a full stack software engineer seeking employment near Los Angeles or remotely. I have a bachelor's degree in computer engineering from UC Santa Cruz and several years of professional software experience. I have experience programming nearly every layer of software from embedded operating systems up to graphical user interfaces. Most recently I have focused on full stack web development with HTML/CSS, Javascript, PHP, and MySQL. I can code almost any front end layout or animation using vanilla HTML, CSS, and Javascript, or with frameworks. On the back end I can create many applications with PHP and MySQL such as login systems, e-commerce sites, and content management systems. I have some experience using Laravel, Wordpress, Angular, Node.

I greatly value self discipline and the ability to learn with minimal direction, particularly with reverse engineering new technologies quickly. In addition I can teach anything I learn to new employees with varying backgrounds. I developed a passion for teaching complex technical ideas in clear and concise language during my 2 years tutoring Java and C for the School of Engineering at UC Santa Cruz.

I am also experienced with technical writing, presentations, graphic design, team leadership, photography, video/audio/image editing.
