BACKGROUND

I am a full stack software engineer seeking employment near Los Angeles or remotely.

I view software as the most powerful tool we have ever created for communication and artistic expression. I am driven by a passion for visual design and an endless curiosity for learning how things work. I have experience programming every layer of software from hardware interfacing to front end web design. I believe that the key to innovation is developing a strong artistic intuition by exposing oneself to a wide variety of creative disciplines, both technical and non-technical.

I have a bachelor's degree in computer engineering and several years of professional software experience. Most recently I have focused on full stack web development. I can design and program almost any responsive front end layout using vanilla HTML, CSS, and Javascript, or with frameworks. On the back end I can create many applications with PHP and MySQL such as login systems, e-commerce sites, content management systems, and most recently Resftul API's. I have some experience using Laravel, Wordpress, Angular, Node. My experience with C, C++ and Java provides a strong foundation for application development using any language.

I greatly value self discipline and the ability to learn with minimal direction, particularly when reverse engineering new technologies. I developed a passion for teaching complex technical ideas in simple language during two years of tutoring Java and C at UC Santa Cruz. I have applied that skill to team leadership, mentoring employees, technical writing, and inspiring people to learn how to program. I am also experienced with graphic design, photography, and multi-media.
